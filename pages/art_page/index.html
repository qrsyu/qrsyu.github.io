Motus VR

Motus VR is a company renowned for its innovative virtual reality locomotion platforms, and ROVR Systems Ltd makes a naturally intuitive locomotion platform for navigating virtual reality (the ROVR), making VR comfortable and accessible for 4yrs to 80+yrs. It is utilized in various sectors including healthcare, training, and gaming. During my internship, I contributed to the development of a 3D environment for an Endless Running game using Unreal Engine 4.27, and it provided me with the opportunity to apply my art design skills in VR environments construction.
